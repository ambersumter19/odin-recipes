Brown Stew Chicken Recipe







Description


This Caribbean Inspired Brown Stew Chicken recipe is so rich and flavorful. I typically serve over my homemade Jamaican Rice and Peas or plain white rice.

Ingredients


  • Chicken Thighs
  • Chicken Buillion Powder
  • Water
  • Ground Thyme
  • Ground Allspice
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Kitchen Bouquet Liquid
  • Ketchup
  • Onions
  • Bell Peppers
  • Fresh Garlic
  • Fresh Ginger
  • Optional - Potatoes and/or Carrots



Steps

  1. Season chicken with salt, pepper, thyme, and allspice. Coat with Kitchen Bouquet and combine well, making sure to reach all of the chicken's crevices very well.
  2. Chop vegetables and combine with seasonings and Kitchen Bouquet and set aside.
  3. Add 2 tsp oil of choice to large heavy bottomed pan and heat. Brown chicken on each side for 3-4 minutes.
  4. Remove chicken and set aside. Add vegetables to pan and brown for about 5-6 minutes.
  5. Return chicken to pan with vegetables and add water, ketchup, chicken powder, and additional seasonings if desired.
  6. Bring to boil and then simmer for about 45 minutes, or until the chicken is falling of the bone. Enjoy!
